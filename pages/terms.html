Terms of Service

Welcome to the Autistic Figure Skating Club! By participating in our activities, you agree to these terms.

1. Code of Conduct

Have Fun, But respect the rink. Treat all skaters, coaches, and families with kindness and inclusion.

2. Safety

All coaches hold First Aid Certificates. The ice is kept clear with no objects. Skaters skate at their own risk. Beginners (Canskate) MUST wear a helmet.

3. Waiver

This club is not a serious competitive organization – it's made for fun. There is no formal liability waiver. By showing up, you acknowledge that skating involves inherent risks, and you participate voluntarily.

4. Attendance & Weather

Coming to the rink or lessons is optional. Weather does not matter – we skate unless announced otherwise on the website or email.

5. Photo/Video

By default, no photos or videos are taken. If you want media of yourself or your child, you must email us to request it.

6. Volunteer & Coaching

Anyone can join as a coach, volunteer, or helper. Just show up on Tuesday or email us.

7. Changes to Terms

We may update these terms at any time. Continued participation means you accept the changes.
