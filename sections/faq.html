Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear to skating? ▼
Reasonable clothing. Recommended: gloves, figure skates, long pants. Beginners (Canskate) MUST wear a helmet.
Do I need my own skates? ▼
Yes! We do not provide skates. You must bring your own.
Is there a quiet space if I get overwhelmed? ▼
Yes, there are quiet spaces available at the rink.
Can parents stay and watch? ▼
Yes, parents can stay or leave – whatever works for you.
How do cancellations get announced? ▼
Cancellations are announced on this website and via email.
Is there a make-up policy for missed sessions? ▼
No policy – you can come or not, it's completely fine.
Are there rental skates? ▼
No, you need your own skates.
How do I sign a waiver? ▼
No formal waiver – this club is just for fun! You can also meet us in person on Tuesdays. Here is our optional Google Form: Club Info Form
Can I volunteer or coach? ▼
Absolutely! Anyone can join our club as a coach, volunteer, or anything. Just come on Tuesday or email us.
What is the photo/video policy? ▼
By default, we do NOT take photos or videos. Parents or participants can email us to request photos/videos of themselves/their child.
