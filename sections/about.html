Our Mission

To create a safe, welcoming, and adaptive space for autistic skaters of all ages and skill levels. We focus on personal growth, community building, and love of the sport.

All coaches have First Aid Certificates. The ice is kept clear with no objects – just smooth ice for everyone.

This club is completely free. Made for fun, by skaters who care.

Quick Facts

  • ⛸️ FREE for all skaters
  • 🧊 Clear ice, no obstacles
  • 🩺 First Aid certified coaches
  • 🏆 All levels welcome
