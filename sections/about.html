About Our Club

Our Mission

To create a safe, welcoming, and adaptive space for autistic skaters of all ages and skill levels. We focus on personal growth, community building, and love of the sport.

This club was founded with one belief: skating should be free, fun, and accessible to everyone. No pressure. No expensive fees. Just ice, friends, and the joy of gliding.

All our coaches hold First Aid Certificates, and the ice is kept completely clear of objects to ensure a sensory-friendly environment.

“Made for fun – not for profit. Everyone is welcome here.”

Quick Facts

  • ✅ FREE for all skaters – always
  • ✅ Every Tuesday at 6:45 AM
  • ✅ Booster Juice Rec Centre (Rink C, Terwillegar)
  • ✅ All coaches have First Aid Certificates
  • ✅ Clear ice – no objects or obstacles
  • ✅ 5 skill levels from Beginner to Senior
  • ✅ Beginners (Canskate) must wear a helmet
  • ✅ No rental skates – bring your own
  • ✅ Quiet spaces available at the rink
  • ✅ Parents can stay or leave – whatever works
