Our Mission & Vision

Skating is for everyone.

We believe that autistic athletes deserve a space where they can learn, grow, and express themselves on ice – without financial barriers or judgment.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide free, inclusive, and adapted figure skating programs for autistic individuals of all ages and skill levels. We remove financial, sensory, and social barriers to make skating accessible to everyone who wants to try.

👁️

Our Vision

A world where every autistic person can experience the joy of skating in a supportive community – and where "made for fun" is the only rule that matters.

Our Core Values

❤️
Fun First

We skate because it's joyful. No competition pressure, just personal growth and smiles.

🧩
Autism Inclusion

Coaches trained in neurodiversity. Quiet spaces available. No sensory overload.

🆓
Free for All

Zero cost. No hidden fees. Skating should never depend on your wallet.

🤲
Volunteer Spirit

Run by volunteers who care. Anyone can contribute as a coach or helper.

"Not serious – just fun. But serious about inclusion."

– Autistic Figure Skating Club founding principle
